Calculation Idea

Implied chance is the latest traded price for the outcome.

Flow bias compares buy volume and sell volume for that outcome.

Estimated chance adjusts price by recent flow and volume confirmation.

flow_bias = (buy_volume - sell_volume) / total_volume
estimated_chance = price + flow_bias * 12% + volume_bonus
edge = estimated_chance - price
score = edge + volume_strength + max_trade_strength

This is a lightweight heuristic. It does not include outside news, settlement rules, liquidity depth, market-maker hedging, or whether a large trade was informed.
